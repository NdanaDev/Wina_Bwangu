Welcome to Wina Bwangu

Wina Bwangu is a leading fintech company offering mobile financial services in various locations across Lusaka. Our services include Airtel Money, MTN Money, Zamtel Money, Zanaco, and FNB, tailored to meet the needs of our diverse clients.

Services Offered

  • Airtel Money: Available at all locations.
  • MTN Money: Available at most locations except Wina5.
  • Zamtel Money: Limited to specific booths.
  • Zanaco: Available at all locations.
  • FNB: Available at all locations.
